About Us

We are a team of passionate and experienced paragliding and skydiving instructors who love to share our skills and knowledge with others. We have been flying for over 10 years and have trained hundreds of students from all over the world. We are certified by the International Paragliding Association (IPA) and the United States Parachute Association (USPA).

We offer a range of courses and services for beginners and advanced flyers. Whether you want to try paragliding or skydiving for the first time, or you want to improve your skills and confidence, we have something for you. We provide all the equipment and safety gear you need, as well as transportation and accommodation if required.

We are based in Muğla Göcek, Turkey, a charming town on the Mediterranean coast. We fly in some of the most scenic and diverse spots in the region, such as Fethiye, Ölüdeniz, Kaş and more. We also organize trips to other amazing locations around the Mediterranean Sea, such as Greece, Italy, Spain and Morocco. We love to explore new destinations and cultures, and we invite you to join us on our next adventure. Contact us today and let us help you make your dreams come true.
